A poisoning attack is when malicious data is introduced into the training dataset to manipulate the learning process of the agent.
In our trading environment, we injected misleading market data during the training phase to degrade the agent's performance.
Due to many factors, including attempting to reduce noise, the poisoning did not have the desired effect.
